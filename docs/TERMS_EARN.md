### English (for international users):

**About Earning 88INF Tokens**

The 88inf Earn feature is NOT cryptocurrency mining. Your device performs no computational work, and no mining software runs in the background. Your device's CPU, GPU, battery, or data are not used for any mining activity.

You earn 88INF tokens as a reward for your engagement with the 88inf platform, including:
- Viewing advertisements served by third-party ad networks
- Completing surveys provided by third-party survey platforms
- Daily check-in activity
- Referring new users to the platform

Token rewards are funded entirely by advertising revenue received by 88inf Ltd. from third-party advertisers. The visual "mining" interface is a gamification element designed to represent your earnings activity and does not reflect any real computational process.

88INF tokens are utility tokens on the Solana blockchain. They are not securities, investment contracts, or financial instruments. Past token value does not guarantee future value. Earning tokens does not constitute an investment.
